    What is GosuBet?

    The GosuBet is a non-money betting system. Everyone starts with 50 StarCraft minerals, 50 WarCraft gold, 50 DotA tangos and 50 DotA 2 branches and are able to put their minerals into many different bets, with odds for each player.

    Depending on the odds, members may win different amounts of minerals. Each season has either a time limit or a mineral goal, and once the season is over, the winner is announced, and sometimes awarded a prize. You must have membership on the site to be given 50 minerals/gold/tangos/branches (depending on the section). Just click on a bet, select how much you'd like to bet, and on whom.

    If you ever lose your minerals/gold/tangos/branches, just click 'Reset GosuBet' and you restart from 50.
